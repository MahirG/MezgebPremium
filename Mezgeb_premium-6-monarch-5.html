Welcome to Mezgeb
Your daily business ledger — track sales, issue VAT receipts, manage customer credit, and accept M-Pesa payments. All in your pocket.
Record Sales in Seconds
Tap the + button, enter the amount, pick a category, and hit Save. Your balance updates instantly. Support for cash, M-Pesa, credit, and bank payments.
Issue VAT Receipts
Check "Generate VAT receipt" when recording a sale. Mezgeb auto-numbers each receipt, calculates 15% VAT, and lets you print or share via WhatsApp and Telegram.
Track Customer Credit
Use the Dube tab to track who owes you. Add customers, record credit and payments, and get alerts when balances are overdue. Never lose track of a debt again.
